# Marketing Kit — SignalTrust AI

Essentials for launch and promotion.

## Value Proposition
- Real-time AI market scanning across crypto, stocks, whales, and news with guardrails.
- Actionable alerts, limits, and payment safeguards to reduce risk.
- Multi-agent architecture running on Render for reliability and speed.

## Target Personas
- Active traders needing faster signal detection.
- Analysts looking for consolidated insights.
- Teams requiring safety controls (limits, permissions, auditability).

## Core Messages & Hooks
- “See signals before the crowd — multi-agent intelligence 24/7.”
- “Built-in guardrails: enforced limits, payment protections, and rate controls.”
- “One stream for crypto, stocks, and whale movements with verified alerts.”

## Assets & Usage
- Logos: `/static/images/` (SVG/PNG). Use primary lockup on light background.
- Colors: Primary `#0A5CFF`, Accent `#10B981`, Dark `#0B1221`, Light `#F5F7FB`.
- Typography: Prefer Sans (e.g., Inter). Keep clearspace around logo.
- Screens: dashboard insights, alert examples, payment guardrails.

## CTAs
- Pre-launch: Join waitlist / Discord.
- Launch: Start trial / Try live demo.
- Post-onboarding: Upgrade plan / Enable alerts.

## Boilerplate
“SignalTrust AI is an AI-powered market scanner that unifies crypto, stocks, whale activity, and news signals into one guarded stream. Built with multi-agent intelligence, payment safeguards, and enforced limits to keep teams fast and safe.”
